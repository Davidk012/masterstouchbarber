Meet Your Master Barber

Dru-Lee Thomas

With over 4 years of experience in the art of barbering, Dru-Lee Thomas brings exceptional skill and passion to every haircut. Guided by his faith, Dru-Lee sees his work as a ministry of service, treating each client with the care and respect they deserve as creations of God.

"The Lord does not look at the things people look at. People look at the outward appearance, but the Lord looks at the heart." 1 Samuel 16:7. At Masters Touch, we strive to enhance your outward appearance while recognizing the inherent worth within every person.

Located in the heart of Johannesburg, Masters Touch Barbers combines traditional techniques with modern styles to create the perfect look for every gentleman. Our shop is designed as a sanctuary where clients can find respite from the world while receiving exceptional grooming services.
