Getting Here

Convenient transportation options

Public Bus

Routes 12, 45, and 78 stop within 2 blocks of our location. Johannesburg Bus Service runs every 20 minutes.

Train

Westbury Station is a 10-minute walk away. Gautrain services connect to all major areas of Johannesburg.

Driving

Easy access from N1 and M1 highways. Free parking available behind our building and on the street.

Ride Share

Uber and Bolt services are readily available throughout Johannesburg. Use our address for drop-off.
