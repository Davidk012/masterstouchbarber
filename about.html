Our Story & Mission

Discover the passion and purpose behind Masters Touch Barbers

"For we are God's handiwork, created in Christ Jesus to do good works" Ephesians 2:10

Meet Your Master Barber

Dru-Lee Thomas

With over 4 years of experience in the art of barbering, Dru-Lee Thomas brings exceptional skill and passion to every haircut. Guided by his faith, Dru-Lee sees his work as a ministry of service, treating each client with the care and respect they deserve as creations of God.

"The Lord does not look at the things people look at. People look at the outward appearance, but the Lord looks at the heart." 1 Samuel 16:7. At Masters Touch, we strive to enhance your outward appearance while recognizing the inherent worth within every person.

"Barbering is not just a profession for me, it's a calling. Every time I pick up my clippers, I'm reminded that I'm serving God by helping people look and feel their best."

- Dru-Lee Thomas, Founder

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to a place of service

2020

The Calling

After feeling called to serve through barbering, Dru-Lee began his apprenticeship at a local barbershop, honing his skills and discovering his passion for the craft.

"Commit your work to the Lord, and your plans will be established"
Proverbs 16:3

2021

First Chair

Dru-Lee earned his barber's license and began working full-time, developing his signature style and building a loyal clientele who appreciated both his skill and spiritual approach.

2022

Vision Takes Shape

The vision for Masters Touch Barbers was born - a place where skill meets spirit, where men could not only get a great haircut but also experience genuine care and community.

2023

Grand Opening

Masters Touch Barbers opened its doors in Johannesburg, with a dedication service to bless the space and commit the business to God's purpose.

"Unless the Lord builds the house, the builders labor in vain"
Psalm 127:1

2025

Expanding Impact

Today, Masters Touch serves hundreds of clients each month, with plans to expand mentorship programs for young barbers seeking to integrate faith and craft.

Our Mission

More than just haircuts

Serving with Excellence, Honoring God

At Masters Touch Barbers, our mission is to provide exceptional grooming services while creating a sanctuary where men can experience God's love through our service. We believe that barbering is a ministry that allows us to:

  • Restore dignity and confidence through quality grooming
  • Create a space for authentic conversation and fellowship
  • Serve each client as if serving Christ Himself
  • Provide mentorship to young men in our community
  • Use our resources to support local ministries and outreach

"Each of you should use whatever gift you have received to serve others, as faithful stewards of God's grace in its various forms." 1 Peter 4:10
